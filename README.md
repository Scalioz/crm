# Scalioz CRM

**Bespoke CRM systems for lead tracking, sales pipelines, WhatsApp follow-up and automated reporting — built for Indian businesses.**

Built by [Scalioz](https://scalioz.com) — a Chennai-based AI automation and digital growth agency.

## What Is Scalioz CRM

Scalioz CRM is a custom CRM platform built specifically for Indian businesses — with WhatsApp integration, INR-based deal tracking, regional language support and automation built for the way Indian sales teams actually work.

## Live Platform

Access the CRM at **[crm.scalioz.com](https://crm.scalioz.com)**

## Core Features

- **Lead Pipeline** — Visual Kanban pipeline tracking leads from enquiry to closed deal
- **WhatsApp Integration** — Every WhatsApp conversation logged automatically in the lead record
- **Follow-up Automation** — Automated reminders, drip sequences and task creation
- **Team Management** — Assign leads to agents, track performance and manage territories
- **Reporting Dashboard** — Daily lead volume, conversion rates and revenue forecasting
- **Multi-source Lead Capture** — Facebook ads, Google ads, website forms and WhatsApp all feed into one pipeline
